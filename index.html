⚓ Our Story

Downey's Seafood Tradition.
Crispy Since Day One.

Fish & Chips has been a Downey staple for over 15 years. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most beloved seafood spots in the city. Our secret is no secret at all: fresh ingredients, hand-battered perfection, and recipes that have stood the test of time.

Every piece of fish that leaves our kitchen is battered and fried to order. Our fries are cut and seasoned fresh. Our chowder simmers all morning until it reaches that perfect creamy consistency. We do things the right way because our customers deserve nothing less.

Hand-Battered Family-Owned Fresh Daily

Signature Batter Recipe

Our batter is the result of years of refinement. Light, crispy, and never greasy. It locks in moisture while creating that perfect crunch. One taste and you'll know why it's our most closely guarded secret.

Fresh, Never Frozen

We source the freshest fish and seafood available. No frozen pre-battered portions here. When you order, we batter it fresh. That's the difference you can see, taste, and feel with every bite.

Community Favorite

We're proud to be part of the Downey community. From sponsoring local events to feeding families, we love giving back to the neighborhood that has supported us for over 15 years.
