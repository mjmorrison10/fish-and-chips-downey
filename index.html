Menu Highlights

Fresh Seafood &
Golden Favorites

Every item on our menu is made to order with the freshest ingredients. Hand-battered, perfectly seasoned, and served piping hot. This is seafood done right.

Classic Fish & Chips

Our signature hand-battered cod, fried to a perfect golden brown. Served with a generous portion of crispy fries and tangy tartar sauce. The dish that started it all.

Shrimp & Chips

Plump, juicy shrimp coated in our signature batter and fried until perfectly crispy. Paired with golden fries and cocktail sauce. A shrimp lover's dream come true.

Combo Seafood Plates

Can't decide? Get the best of everything. Fish, shrimp, and your choice of additional seafood on one loaded plate. Perfect for those who want it all.

Grilled Fish Tacos

Fresh grilled fish wrapped in warm corn tortillas with crisp cabbage, pico de gallo, and our house cream sauce. Light, fresh, and bursting with flavor.

Clam Chowder

Rich, creamy, and loaded with tender clams and potatoes. Our New England-style clam chowder is made fresh daily. Perfect as a starter or a meal on its own.

Fried Oysters

Fresh oysters lightly breaded and fried until golden. Crispy on the outside, tender and briny on the inside. Served with lemon and house-made remoulade.

Coleslaw & Sides

Creamy coleslaw, crispy onion rings, seasoned rice, garlic bread, and more. Every great seafood meal deserves the perfect side. Build your perfect plate.

Family Packs

Feed the whole crew with our family-sized combos. Multiple pieces of fish, shrimp, fries, and sides. Perfect for family dinners, game day, or any gathering.

About Fish & Chips

Downey's Seafood Tradition.
Crispy Since Day One.

Fish & Chips has been a Downey staple for over 15 years. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the most beloved seafood spots in the city. Our secret is no secret at all: fresh ingredients, hand-battered perfection, and recipes that have stood the test of time.

Every piece of fish that leaves our kitchen is battered and fried to order. Our fries are cut and seasoned fresh. Our chowder simmers all morning until it reaches that perfect creamy consistency. We do things the right way because our customers deserve nothing less.

Whether you are a lifelong Downey resident or just passing through, we invite you to taste what keeps people coming back. One bite of our crispy, golden fish and you will understand why we are Downey's favorite.

Hand-Battered Family-Owned Fresh Daily

Signature Batter Recipe

Our batter is the result of years of refinement. Light, crispy, and never greasy. It locks in moisture while creating that perfect crunch. One taste and you will know why it is our most closely guarded secret.

Fresh, Never Frozen

We source the freshest fish and seafood available. No frozen pre-battered portions here. When you order, we batter it fresh. That is the difference you can see, taste, and feel with every bite.

Community Favorite

We are proud to be part of the Downey community. From sponsoring local events to feeding families, we love giving back to the neighborhood that has supported us for over 15 years.
